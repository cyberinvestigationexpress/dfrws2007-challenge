Microsoft's Big (Mango) Mobile Moment

Here comes Mango. The next version of Microsoft's Windows Phone operating system, Mango was previewed at Mix 11 and is expected to get a big push in coming weeks, whether at TechEd next or in a possible curtain-raising event later this month. Regardless, some new features began to leak out this week and not a moment too soon because what's been shown so far might be a bit too under-the-hood to generate the excitement the platform needs (See: A MIXed Bag: A Look at Microsoft's MIX11 Mobile Announcements).

On tap in Mango, according to reports today: a Shazam-like music discovery service called Bing Audio; an augmented reality app dubbed Bing Vision; as well as other new features including QR Code support, turn-by-turn voice navigation, SMS voice dictation and more. We have several looks at Microsoft's mobile OS progress below. Check them out.

Also in this issue: a talk with mobile developer Five Mobile; the latest numbers showing Apple still on top in mobile; and an even bigger number forecasting 44 BILLION app downloads by 2016.

Tks for reading.
