Blend Hosted and Local Desktop Virtualization to Better Meet User Needs
On-Demand | Seminars on Your Schedule

Serving a diverse workforce? You have more choices than just hosted VDI

While hosted desktop virtualization undoubtedly has its benefits, it’s not always the easiest or most cost-effective way to deliver services to users. By leveraging the power of different technologies – Terminal Server, application virtualization, local VDI, and others – organizations can better meet the needs of their users while remaining within their budget.

This on-demand web seminar explains how you can:

  • enable Mac, Linux, and mobile users to access corporate Windows desktops and applications securely
  • safely initiate a Bring Your Own Computer (BYOC) program
  • make platform and protocol changes without changing the desktop management structure

Take advantage of all virtualization has to offer – view this seminar now!
